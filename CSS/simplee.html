Articles in English

In English grammar there are three articles A,An and The.we use
articles with nouns.
e.g ! bought a pen.


A -With singular,countable nouns which Starts with consonant sound
e.g. a boy, a car, a tree,a book,a chair, a university, etc.


An-with singular,countable nouns which starts with vowel(a,e,i,o,u) sound. e.g. an apple, an elephant, an ice-cream,an orange, an umbrella, an hour , an intelligent boy, an MBA, etc.


The - 1.Specific reference 2.Unique things 3.with superoative forms
4.with the names of rivers,mountain ranges ,ship names ,holy books, etc.
5.To talk about entire species or class.
